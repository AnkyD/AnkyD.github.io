At Karibo Cosmetics, we prioritize quality over quantity and hence, invest time and effort to create the best customized lipsticks for you! We ensure that our development process is organic, safe and transparent - as a result, what you see is what you get! We are on a mission to blend cosmetics with skincare - Join us!

It was a coffee break that led founders Bhakti Bhanushali and Jasmine Shah to conceptualize Karibo. Being in the corporate sector, makeup was a part and parcel of their life but our founders didn’t miss out that the conventional products were doing more harm than good. So to see the change, they became the change with Karibo’s line of custom made and organic lipsticks.

At Karibo, we believe that beauty should be distinct and diverse - with no set shade, shape or outline. Our motto is to create beauty organically and therefore, only use natural elements

FAQs

How long does a consultation take?

A detailed consultation takes about 20 minutes.

What happens during the consultation?

During the consultation, we work on the colours that match your personality, undertones, and skin colour keeping in mind your colour preference. With the final outcome, we derive a colour as per your want.

Can I make group bookings?

We can cater for group bookings and have facilities for a minimum of 3 people. We also take events appointments like birthday, kittyparty, weddings, and other such events
